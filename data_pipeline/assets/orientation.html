What to look at:
- pitch / roll are stable when the device is mounted in a fixed pose. Small std = good.
- yaw (device) uses the magnetometer and is often noisy in vehicles. We compare it to heading from PPK velocity (from vn,ve). If the offset is constant, the device yaw is usable; if random, magnetometer is unreliable.
- yaw - heading approximates the boresight between camera-forward and device-forward when moving.
