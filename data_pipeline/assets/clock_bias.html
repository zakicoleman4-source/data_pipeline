epochs: __N_EPOCHS__ span: __SPAN_S__ s leap_s: __LEAP_S__ sys−GPS median: __SYS_MED_MS__ ms sys−GPS std: __SYS_STD_MS__ ms drift: __DRIFT_PPB__ ppb HW disc.: __DISCS__
sys−GPS = device Android system clock minus the GNSS-chip-derived UTC ((TimeNanos − FullBiasNanos − BiasNanos)/1e9 + GPS_EPOCH_POSIX − leap_s). It is the offset an app would see when stamping data with System.currentTimeMillis(). Small (< 50 ms) = device NTP-disciplined. Large or step-shaped = OS clock corrections.
drift = how fast the GNSS hardware counter slips relative to GPS time; median DriftNanosPerSecond converted to ppb. Stable devices sit at ±50 ppb.
HW disc. = changes in HardwareClockDiscontinuityCount; each jump means the chip's monotonic counter reset and bias re-locked.
